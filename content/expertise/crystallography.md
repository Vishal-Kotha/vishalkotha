### Structural Fingerprinting
Understanding the atomic arrangement is the first step in explaining material behavior. I employ **X-Ray Diffraction (XRD)** not just for phase identification, but for deep structural analysis using **Rietveld Refinement**.

### Key Capabilities
* **Phase Identification:** Distinguishing between polymorphic forms (e.g., Hexagonal vs. Monoclinic $WO_3$).
* **Strain Analysis:** Calculating lattice strain induced by dopant substitution.
* **Crystallite Size Estimation:** Utilizing the Scherrer equation to correlate synthesis parameters with grain growth.
